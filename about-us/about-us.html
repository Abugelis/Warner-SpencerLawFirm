Welcome to Warner & Spencer Law Firm

John O’Leary Solicitors is a dedicated team of experienced and approachable solicitors based in the heart of the city. We are proud to provide clear, practical legal advice across a broad range of practice areas, with particular expertise in personal injury, medical negligence, and complex civil claims. Whatever legal challenge you may be facing, our aim is to deliver reliable guidance and strong representation when it matters most.

We recognise that seeking legal advice can feel overwhelming. Legal issues often arise during stressful or uncertain moments — whether you are dealing with a family matter, a workplace dispute, a property transaction, or an unexpected injury. Our role is to support you through every stage of the process, explaining your options in plain language and ensuring you feel informed, confident, and supported at all times.

By continually investing in our people, our facilities, and modern legal technologies, we are able to offer a highly efficient and responsive service without losing the personal approach our clients value. We combine technical excellence with genuine care, ensuring each case receives the attention it deserves.

Our reputation has been built on achieving strong results while remaining approachable, transparent, and cost-conscious. Today, we act for a diverse client base including individuals, families, and businesses, both locally and beyond. Through our insights and updates, we also share commentary on legal developments and matters of public interest relevant to our clients.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our full range of legal services, including personal injury claims, family law, wills and estate planning, residential and commercial property, employment matters, and business law.

For updates, legal insights, and firm news, you can also connect with us on social media.

We look forward to working with you and providing the legal support you can rely on.
