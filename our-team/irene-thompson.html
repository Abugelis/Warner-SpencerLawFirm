Irene Thompson

Irene Thompson is a Solicitor at Warner & Spencer, bringing a thoughtful, client-focused approach to a wide range of legal matters. She is known for her professionalism, empathy, and commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for those she represents.

Irene advises clients on areas including family law, probate and estate planning, residential conveyancing, and general civil matters. She understands that many legal issues arise during sensitive or challenging times, and she takes care to guide clients through the process with clarity, discretion, and reassurance.

With a strong emphasis on communication and preparation, Irene ensures that clients fully understand their options before making important decisions. Her measured and detail-oriented working style has earned her the trust of both clients and colleagues alike.

Irene is a valued member of the firm, contributing to its reputation for providing practical, dependable, and compassionate legal advice.
